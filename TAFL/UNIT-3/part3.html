В§9 Simplification of CFG

1. Why Simplify?

A CFG may contain unnecessary productions that complicate analysis but don't affect the language. Simplification removes these, making the grammar cleaner for Normal Form conversion.

Three types of simplifications:
в‘  Eliminating Оө-productions    в‘Ў Eliminating Unit Productions    в‘ў Eliminating Useless Productions

2. Eliminating Оө-Productions

A variable A is nullable if A вҮ’* Оө (can derive empty string).

An Оө-production is a rule of the form A вҶ’ Оө.
Algorithm to remove Оө-productions:
  • Step 1: Find all nullable variables (those that can derive Оө)
  • Step 2: For each production, add new versions with each nullable variable either present or absent
  • Step 3: Remove all Оө-productions (except SвҶ’Оө if Оө вҲҲ L(G))
Q вҖ” Remove Оө-productions: SвҶ’ABC; AвҶ’BC|a; BвҶ’bAC|Оө; CвҶ’CAB|Оө
Step 1 вҖ” Find nullable variables: BвҶ’Оө вң“, CвҶ’Оө вң“, AвҶ’BC (B,C both nullable) вҶ’ A nullable вң“, SвҶ’ABC (all nullable) вҶ’ S nullable вң“
Step 2 вҖ” Generate new productions for SвҶ’ABC (A,B,C all nullable):
S вҶ’ ABC | BC | AC | AB | A | B | C | Оө
A вҶ’ BC | a | C | B
B вҶ’ bAC | bA | bC | b
C вҶ’ CAB | CA | CB | AB | C | A | B
(Remove all XвҶ’Оө entries from final grammar)

3. Eliminating Unit Productions

A unit production is a production of the form A вҶ’ B where both A and B are non-terminals. These can complicate proofs but are sometimes useful in creating unambiguous grammars.
Algorithm to remove unit productions:
  • Step 1: Find all unit pairs (A, B) вҖ” where A вҮ’* B through unit productions
  • Step 2: For each unit pair (A, B): add A вҶ’ Оұ for every B вҶ’ Оұ that is a NON-unit production
  • Step 3: Remove all unit productions A вҶ’ B from the grammar
Q вҖ” Remove unit productions: S вҶ’ Aa|B; B вҶ’ A|bb; A вҶ’ a|bc|B
Unit pairs: (S,B), (B,A), (A,B), (S,A) [transitively via SвҶ’BвҶ’A]
For each pair, add non-unit productions:
(S,B): add SвҶ’bb (from BвҶ’bb)
(B,A): add BвҶ’a|bc (from AвҶ’a|bc)
(A,B): add AвҶ’bb (from BвҶ’bb)
(S,A): add SвҶ’a|bc (from AвҶ’a|bc)

Final grammar (no unit productions):
S вҶ’ Aa | bb | a | bc
B вҶ’ bb | a | bc
A вҶ’ a | bc | bb

4. Eliminating Useless Productions

A symbol X is useful if:
в‘  X is generating: X вҮ’* w (derives some terminal string) AND
в‘Ў X is reachable: S вҮ’* ОұXОІ (reachable from start symbol S)

A symbol that is NOT both generating AND reachable is useless.
Algorithm (4 steps):
  • Step 1: SвӮҒ = set of all terminal symbols (they are generating by definition)
  • Step 2: SвӮӮ = SвӮҒ вҲӘ {NTs that directly derive terminals only} вҶ’ "directly terminates"
  • Step 3: Add NTs whose ALL RHS symbols are in SвӮӮ вҶ’ repeat until stable
  • Step 4: Check reachability from S вҖ” remove any NT not reachable from S. Result = useful symbols only.
Q вҖ” Remove useless: S вҶ’ AB|a; A вҶ’ BC|b; B вҶ’ aB|aC; C вҶ’ abCA|abb; D вҶ’ bD|ac
Step 1: SвӮҒ = {a,b} (terminal symbols)
Step 2: Who directly derives terminals? AвҶ’b вң“, SвҶ’a вң“, DвҶ’ac вң“, CвҶ’abb вң“
Step 3: BвҶ’aC (A and C now in SвӮӮ? C yes, need A in SвӮӮ first... AвҶ’b вң“ so AвҲҲSвӮӮ). BвҶ’aB? B needs B... вҶ’ Actually B derives: BвҶ’aB|aC. Since CвҲҲSвӮӮ and a is terminal: BвҶ’aC вҶ’ BвҲҲSвӮӮ вң“
Generating: S, A, B, C (all derive terminals). D generates ac вң“ too.
Reachable from S: SвҶ’AB: A,B reachable. SвҶ’a. AвҶ’BC: B,C reachable. D not reachable from S!
Remove D (not reachable). Final grammar: SвҶ’AB|a; AвҶ’BC|b; BвҶ’aB|aC; CвҶ’abCA|abb

Q1 вҖ” Remove Оө-productions: S вҶ’ aSB|a|Ab; A вҶ’ Оө; B вҶ’ bBB|Оө
Nullable variables: AвҶ’Оө вң“, BвҶ’Оө вң“. Is S nullable? SвҶ’aSB (S needs itself, not directly). S is not nullable.
New productions (each nullable can be present or absent):
S вҶ’ aSB | aS | aB | a | Ab | b   [A,B both nullable вҶ’ include versions without each]
A вҶ’ (removed вҖ” only had AвҶ’Оө)
B вҶ’ bBB | bB | bB | b   [B nullable: bBBвҶ’bBB|bB]

S вҶ’ aSB | aS | aB | a | Ab | b
B вҶ’ bBB | bB | b
Q2 вҖ” Remove unit productions and useless symbols: S вҶ’ AB; A вҶ’ a; B вҶ’ C; C вҶ’ D; D вҶ’ b
Unit pairs: (B,C), (C,D). Chain: BвҶ’CвҶ’DвҶ’b. Also BвҮ’*b transitively.
Unit productions: BвҶ’C, CвҶ’D вҖ” remove these.
Add: BвҶ’b (from DвҶ’b via chain), CвҶ’b (from DвҶ’b)
Now: SвҶ’AB; AвҶ’a; BвҶ’b; CвҶ’b; DвҶ’b
Check useless: C and D reachable from S? SвҶ’AB, no path to C or D вҶ’ useless!
Final grammar: S вҶ’ AB; A вҶ’ a; B вҶ’ b
Language: {ab}

В§10 Chomsky Normal Form (CNF)

1. Definition of CNF

A CFG is in Chomsky Normal Form (CNF) if every production is of the form:

NT вҶ’ T   (single terminal)
NT вҶ’ NT  NT   (exactly two non-terminals)
OR:  S вҶ’ Оө  if Оө вҲҲ L(G)

where NT = Non-Terminal, T = Terminal.

2. Steps to Convert CFG to CNF

Step 1
Eliminate all Оө-productions (find nullable variables, generate new rules)
Step 2
Eliminate all unit productions (AвҶ’B type)
Step 3
Eliminate all useless symbols and productions
Step 4
For each production A вҶ’ aвӮҒaвӮӮвҖҰaвӮҷ (nвүҘ2): replace each terminal aбөў with a new NT Rбөў, add rule Rбөў вҶ’ aбөў
Step 5
For each remaining production with 3+ NTs, break into binary: AвҶ’XвӮҒXвӮӮвҖҰXвӮҷ becomes AвҶ’XвӮҒYвӮҒ, YвӮҒвҶ’XвӮӮYвӮӮ, вҖҰ, YвӮҷвӮӢвӮӮвҶ’XвӮҷвӮӢвӮҒXвӮҷ

3. CNF Conversion вҖ” Worked Example 1

Convert to CNF: S вҶ’ aAD; A вҶ’ aB; A вҶ’ bAB; B вҶ’ b; D вҶ’ d
Step 1,2,3: No Оө, unit, or useless productions here.
Step 4: Replace terminals in long productions with new NTs
S вҶ’ aAD: 'a' is terminal in 3-symbol RHS вҶ’ introduce RвӮҒвҶ’a
A вҶ’ aB: 'a' is terminal in 2-symbol RHS вҶ’ introduce RвӮҒвҶ’a
A вҶ’ bAB: 'b' is terminal вҶ’ introduce RвӮӮвҶ’b

After step 4:
S вҶ’ RвӮҒAD   (still 3 NTs вҖ” need step 5)
A вҶ’ RвӮҒB    (2 NTs вң“)
A вҶ’ RвӮӮAB   (3 NTs вҖ” need step 5)
RвӮҒ вҶ’ a   RвӮӮ вҶ’ b   B вҶ’ b   D вҶ’ d
Step 5: Break 3-NT productions into binary
S вҶ’ RвӮҒAD вҶ’ S вҶ’ RвӮҒXвӮҒ; XвӮҒ вҶ’ AD
A вҶ’ RвӮӮAB вҶ’ A вҶ’ RвӮӮYвӮҒ; YвӮҒ вҶ’ AB

Final CNF:
S вҶ’ RвӮҒXвӮҒ  |  XвӮҒ вҶ’ AD  |  A вҶ’ RвӮҒB | RвӮӮYвӮҒ  |  YвӮҒ вҶ’ AB
RвӮҒ вҶ’ a  |  RвӮӮ вҶ’ b  |  B вҶ’ b  |  D вҶ’ d

4. CNF Conversion вҖ” Worked Example 2

Convert to CNF: S вҶ’ aAbB; A вҶ’ aA|a; B вҶ’ bB|b (no simplification needed)
S вҶ’ aAbB: has terminals a,b in 4-symbol production. Replace terminals:
Introduce: RвӮҗвҶ’a, R_bвҶ’b
S вҶ’ RвӮҗ A R_b B (4 NTs вҖ” break into binary)

S вҶ’ RвӮҗ XвӮҒ; XвӮҒ вҶ’ A XвӮӮ; XвӮӮ вҶ’ R_b B

A вҶ’ aA: replace a вҶ’ RвӮҗ A (already 2 NTs вң“ after: A вҶ’ RвӮҗ A)
A вҶ’ a: just one terminal вң“ keep: A вҶ’ a... wait, that's NTвҶ’T вң“ (one terminal = CNF вң“)
B вҶ’ bB: B вҶ’ R_b B вң“
B вҶ’ b: terminal only вң“

Final CNF:
S вҶ’ RвӮҗ XвӮҒ      XвӮҒ вҶ’ A XвӮӮ      XвӮӮ вҶ’ R_b B
A вҶ’ RвӮҗ A | a   B вҶ’ R_b B | b
RвӮҗ вҶ’ a         R_b вҶ’ b

5. Practice Questions вҖ” В§10

Q1 вҖ” Convert to CNF: S вҶ’ aAb; A вҶ’ Aa|Оө
First: AвҶ’Оө means A is nullable. Apply Оө-removal, then convert.
Step 1 (Оө-removal): A nullable. SвҶ’aAb вҶ’ add SвҶ’ab (with A absent)
S вҶ’ aAb | ab; A вҶ’ Aa | a (removed AвҶ’Оө)

Step 4 (replace terminals): RвӮҗвҶ’a, R_bвҶ’b
S вҶ’ RвӮҗ A R_b | RвӮҗ R_b; A вҶ’ A RвӮҗ | a

Step 5 (break 3-NTs):
S вҶ’ RвӮҗ XвӮҒ; XвӮҒ вҶ’ A R_b  |  S вҶ’ RвӮҗ R_b вң“
A вҶ’ A RвӮҗ вң“  | A вҶ’ a вң“
CNF: SвҶ’RвӮҗ XвӮҒ|RвӮҗ R_b; XвӮҒвҶ’AR_b; AвҶ’ARвӮҗ|a; RвӮҗвҶ’a; R_bвҶ’b
Q2 вҖ” Is this in CNF? S вҶ’ AB; A вҶ’ a; B вҶ’ bB|b. If not, convert.
Check: SвҶ’AB вң“ (NT NT). AвҶ’a вң“ (single T). BвҶ’bB вҖ” 'b' is terminal in 2-symbol RHS вҶ’ NOT CNF. BвҶ’b вң“.
NOT in CNF. Fix: Introduce R_b вҶ’ b
B вҶ’ R_b B | b вҶҗ now CNF вң“
CNF: SвҶ’AB; AвҶ’a; BвҶ’R_b B|b; R_bвҶ’b

6. CNF вҖ” Advanced Example (Full Pipeline)

Convert to CNF: S вҶ’ aAbB; A вҶ’ aA|a; B вҶ’ bB|b (complete steps)
This grammar has no Оө or unit productions, no useless symbols. Go directly to step 4.
S вҶ’ aAbB: 4 symbols (mixed terminals+NTs)
Introduce: RвӮҒвҶ’a, RвӮӮвҶ’b
Step 4: S вҶ’ RвӮҒARвӮӮB (4 NTs)
A вҶ’ aA вҶ’ RвӮҒA (2 NTs вң“); A вҶ’ a вң“
B вҶ’ bB вҶ’ RвӮӮB (2 NTs вң“); B вҶ’ b вң“

Step 5: S вҶ’ RвӮҒARвӮӮB вҶ’ S вҶ’ RвӮҒX; X вҶ’ ARвӮӮB вҶ’ X вҶ’ AY; Y вҶ’ RвӮӮB

Final CNF:
S вҶ’ RвӮҒX   X вҶ’ AY   Y вҶ’ RвӮӮB
A вҶ’ RвӮҒA | a   B вҶ’ RвӮӮB | b
RвӮҒ вҶ’ a   RвӮӮ вҶ’ b
вҳ… CNF Key Points: Every CFG can be converted to CNF. CNF is used in CYK parsing algorithm. In CNF: parse trees are always binary trees. Max string derivable in exactly n steps has length n (for NTвҶ’T rules).

В§11 Greibach Normal Form (GNF)

1. Definition of GNF

A CFG is in Greibach Normal Form (GNF) if every production is of the form:

NT вҶ’ a K  where aвҲҲT, KвҲҲ(V_N)*
NT вҶ’ T  (single terminal)
OR: S вҶ’ Оө if Оө вҲҲ L(G)

вҳ… Every production starts with exactly one terminal followed by zero or more non-terminals.

2. Removing Left Recursion (needed for GNF)

Left Recursion: A production A вҶ’ AОұ | ОІ is left recursive (A appears at the start of RHS).

To remove: Replace with right recursion:
A вҶ’ AОұ | ОІ   вҹә   A вҶ’ ОІZ; Z вҶ’ ОұZ | Оұ
Remove left recursion: E вҶ’ E+T | T
E вҶ’ TZ    (ОІ = T, move to front)
Z вҶ’ +TZ | +T    (Оұ = +T, create Z for right recursion)
E вҶ’ Tz | T is equivalent: E вҶ’ TZ; Z вҶ’ +TZ | Оө. Without Оө: Z вҶ’ +TZ | +T

3. Steps to Convert to GNF

Step 1
Rename variables as AвӮҒ, AвӮӮ, вҖҰ, AвӮҷ in order
Step 2
For each Aбөў, ensure that all productions Aбөў вҶ’ AвұјОұ have j > i (no left recursion). If Aбөў вҶ’ AвұјОұ and j вүӨ i, substitute Aвұј's productions into it.
Step 3
Remove direct left recursion (using the AвҶ’ОІZ trick)
Step 4
Work backwards: substitute to make every production start with a terminal

4. GNF Worked Example 1

Convert to GNF: S вҶ’ aB|ab; A вҶ’ aAB|a; B вҶ’ ABb|b
Step 1: Rename: SвҶ’AвӮҒ, AвҶ’AвӮӮ, BвҶ’AвӮғ
AвӮҒ вҶ’ aAвӮғ | ab
AвӮӮ вҶ’ aAвӮӮAвӮғ | a
AвӮғ вҶ’ AвӮӮAвӮғb | b

AвӮғ starts with AвӮӮ (j=2 < i=3) вҶ’ substitute AвӮӮ:
AвӮғ вҶ’ (aAвӮӮAвӮғ)AвӮғb | aAвӮғb | b
AвӮғ вҶ’ aAвӮӮAвӮғAвӮғb | aAвӮғb | b вң“ (all start with terminal now)

GNF (rename back):
S вҶ’ aB | ab
A вҶ’ aAB | a
B вҶ’ aABBb | aBb | b вң“ all start with terminal

5. GNF Worked Example 2 вҖ” Left Recursion Removal

Convert to GNF: S вҶ’ SoS|1o1
S вҶ’ SoS is left recursive. Remove using Z trick:
S has: Оұ = oS, ОІ = 1o1
S вҶ’ 1o1Z | 1o1   (ОІ starts every production)
Z вҶ’ oSZ | oS    (right recursive Z)

But 'o' and '1' are terminals. Check GNF form:
S вҶ’ 1 RвӮ’ 1 Z | 1 RвӮ’ 1   (need to handle multi-char terminal sequences)
In simplified notation:
S вҶ’ 01Z | 01
Z вҶ’ 0SZ | 0S   вҶҗ GNF вң“ (starts with terminal 0)

AвӮӮ вҶ’ aAвӮҒZ | bZ | aAвӮҒ | b   (using ОІ = aAвӮҒ|b)

AвӮҒ вҶ’ AвӮӮAвӮӮ | a вҶ’ substitute AвӮӮ:

Common Exam Mistakes to Avoid

  • Mixing right and left linear in SAME grammar вҶ’ NOT Regular (it's Linear/CFG)
  • In LMD: always expand the LEFTMOST variable вҖ” don't skip any
  • In CNF: AвҶ’aB is NOT CNF (terminal in 2-symbol production needs RвӮҗвҶ’a)
  • In GNF: productions must start with EXACTLY ONE terminal
  • Ambiguity requires two DISTINCT LMDs вҖ” not just two different strings
  • Оө-removal: SвҶ’Оө stays if ОөвҲҲL(G), otherwise remove it
  • Useless = NOT generating OR NOT reachable (either condition = useless)
